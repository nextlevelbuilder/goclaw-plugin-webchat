GoClaw WebChat - Customized Example

Custom branding, bottom-left position, programmatic control.

Custom Theme

GoClaw.init({
  url: 'wss://your-server.com/ws',
  token: 'your-token',
  position: 'bottom-left',
  theme: {
    base: 'light',
    primaryColor: '#059669',
    userBubbleColor: '#059669',
    borderRadius: '12px',
    fontFamily: '"Inter", sans-serif',
  },
});
